How to meet the glass recycling obligation

A minimum of 0% of your glass recycling obligation must be made up of glass re-melt PRNs or PERNs. This will make up a proportion of your overall glass recycling obligation. The remaining glass recycling obligation can be met by either glass re-melt, glass other or a mix of both.
